CPT Correlation

This document presents the correlation implemented in the current code. ## Basic Engineering Properties

Unit Weight

Robertson (2010)


γ/γw = 0.27[log (Rf)] + 0.36[log (qt/pa)] + 1.236
1

  • γ = unit weight of soil
  • γw = unit weight of water
  • Rf = friction ratio, the ratio of fs over qt, i.e, $\frac{f_s}{q_t} \times \text{100\%}$
  • qt = corrected cone resistance
  • Pa = atmospheric pressure

Relative Density

Jamiolkowski (2003), Table 5 on Page 9. The original form of relative density can be expressed as
$$ D_R = \frac{1}{C_2}$$


$$ D_r = \frac{1}{3.10}\cdot \ln\bigg[\frac{q_t/P_a}{17.68\cdot(\sigma_{v0}'/P_a))^{0.5}}\bigg]$$

Friction Angle


$$\varphi_p'= 17.6+11\cdot\log_{10}\bigg[\frac{q_t/P_a}{(\sigma_{v0}'/P_a)^{0.5}}\bigg]$$

Small Strain Stiffness G0


G0 = 50 ⋅ σatm[(qt − σv0/σatm]m*
where:
$$ m^* = \begin{cases} 0.6 & \text{sand} \\ 0.8 & \text{silt} \\ 1.0 & \text{clay} \end{cases} $$
